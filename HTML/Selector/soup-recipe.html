Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • vegetable broth
  • corn
  • celery
  • carrots
  • egg noodles
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Chop celery and carrots into bite-size pieces for soup.
  2. Remove corn kernal from corn cob.
  3. Add vegetables and noodles to vegetable broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil. Cook until vegetables are tender and noodles are cooked through.
