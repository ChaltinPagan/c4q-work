Halloween Costumes

Pirate Costumes

For a pirate costume you will need: eye patch, a hat with a skull and cross bones, and a parrot.

Detective Costume

For a detective costume, you will need: a fedora, a trench coat, and a magnifying glass.

Fairy Costumes

For a fairy costume, you will need: wings, a wand, and lots of glitter.

Pumpkin Apprentice

To be a pumpkin apprentice, you will need: a name tag that says "Pumpkin Apprentice: Your Name" and a love of pumpkins.
